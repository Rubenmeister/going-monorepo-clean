## ✅ STYLE GUIDE (for writers)

Create and share this document with all hired writers:

````markdown
# GOING PLATFORM - CONTENT STYLE GUIDE

## Brand Voice

- Professional but approachable
- Helpful and empowering
- Inclusive and community-focused
- Clear and jargon-free

## Tone Examples

### For Blog Posts

"Want to earn more as a Going driver? Here are our favorite money-making tips..."
(Conversational, empowering)

### For Help Articles

"To update your profile: 1. Open app 2. Tap Account 3. Select Edit Profile"
(Clear, step-by-step)

### For About Page

"Founded in 2020, Going Platform connects communities through safe, reliable transportation."
(Professional, mission-focused)

## Writing Guidelines

### Structure

- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use subheadings every 200-300 words
- Bullet points for lists
- One idea per paragraph

### Readability

- Target 8th-grade reading level
- Average sentence: 15-20 words
- Avoid jargon (or explain it)
- Use active voice

### Format

- **Bold** for key terms (first mention)
- _Italics_ for emphasis (rare)
- CTA buttons in CAPS: "START LEARNING"
- Links as [anchor text](url)

### SEO

- Include target keyword naturally in:
  - Title (H1)
  - First 100 words
  - Subheadings (H2/H3)
  - Meta description (150-160 chars)

### Examples

**For H1:** "How to Maximize Your Earnings as a Going Driver"
**For meta:** "Learn proven strategies to boost your income as a Going driver. Daily tips, best practices, and insider advice."
**For intro:** "Want to earn more on the Going platform? We've compiled..."

## Checklists

### Before Submitting

- [ ] Grammar checked (Grammarly free OK)
- [ ] Spell-checked
- [ ] Links tested
- [ ] Tone matches guidelines
- [ ] Subheadings every 300 words
- [ ] SEO keyword included 3-5 times
- [ ] Clear CTA at end
- [ ] 8th-grade reading level
- [ ] No orphaned sentences

### Word Counts

- Blog posts: 1500-2500 words
- Help articles: 300-800 words
- About page: 1500 words
- Service descriptions: 500+ words
- Course content: 500+ words
- News articles: 500-800 words

### File Format

Save as: `[TITLE-SLUG].md`

Example: `top-10-tips-safe-driving.md`

Include at top:

```markdown
---
title: 'Top 10 Tips for Safe Driving on Going Platform'
author: 'Your Name'
date: '2026-02-21'
category: 'Tips'
readTime: '5 min'
seoDescription: 'Learn proven strategies to stay safe while using Going Platform...'
---
```
````

```
## 💡 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Be clear on expectations:** Word count, deadline, tone, format
2. **Communicate daily:** Stand-ups keep everyone aligned
3. **Give quick feedback:** 24-hour turnaround on edits
4. **Provide context:** Share brand guide, user personas, examples
5. **Be flexible:** Some writers work better with templates, others need freedom
6. **Track progress:** Daily word count tracking helps with timeline
7. **Build relationship:** Treat writers well - they may help with future projects
8. **Have backups:** If one writer gets sick, have contingency plans
9. **Pay promptly:** For freelancers, quick payment is crucial
10. **Celebrate wins:** When content goes live, celebrate the team
```
