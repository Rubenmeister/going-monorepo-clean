## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Respond Quickly**

   - Reply to applications same day
   - Schedule interviews within 24 hours
   - Make offers within 1 hour of decision
   - → Writers get nervous if delayed

2. **Be Clear on Expectations**

   - Share project brief upfront
   - Show deadline calendar
   - Discuss payment terms early
   - → Prevents surprises later

3. **Value Their Time**

   - Don't waste time with rejected candidates
   - Be upfront about timeline
   - Offer payment for writing tests
   - → Shows professionalism

4. **Build Quick Rapport**

   - Use their name frequently
   - Ask about their background
   - Show genuine interest
   - → They'll want to work with you

5. **Mention Growth Opportunity**
   - "This could lead to ongoing work"
   - "Portfolio builder for your site"
   - "Reference/recommendation letter"
   - → Writers value future opportunities

## ❌ RED FLAGS TO AVOID

Don't hire if they:

- [ ] No portfolio or samples provided
- [ ] Vague about experience
- [ ] Don't meet deadline in first 24 hours
- [ ] Pushy about price without negotiation
- [ ] No communication during application process
- [ ] Negative about revision process
- [ ] Can't commit to timeline
- [ ] Red flags in references (if you check)
